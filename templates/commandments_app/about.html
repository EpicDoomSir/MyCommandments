About Us
MyCommandments.com is an idea launched by a group of friends who
felt that the sacrosanct 613 Commandments was a fairly large number
to deal with. As such they determined to cut the number down to
something more manageable, relevant and personal. The commandments
have been organized according to their best understanding of Jewish
Orthodox tradition. This site is the result of that effort.
Comments, corrections and suggestions are welcome.
